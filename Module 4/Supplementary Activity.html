Mission Statement

Inspired by the virtues of St. Cecilia, we will strive to:

  • provide quality education with spirituality at the core of the curriculum;
  • build a responsible Catholic Christian community by imbuing love and service for others;
  • preserve the integrity of creation and total human development.

Vision Statement

As living witness of the Gospel, Sta. Cecilia Parochial School envisions to harmonize Science and Religion that value life and culture to be a catalyst of conversion who promotes faith, hope, justice and unconditional love.
