Pinoy-Style Chicken Curry

Image from Chicken Curry.

A Rich and Flavorful Filipino Dish

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 tablespoon Curry Powder
  • 1 Potato, cubed
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 thumbs Ginger, cut into strips
  • 2 stalks Celery, sliced
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 2 Long Green Peppers
  • 2 cups Coconut Milk
  • 1 cup Water
  • Fish Sauce and Ground Black Pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Cream (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan. Fry the potato cubes until golden and slightly crispy, then set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© garlic, onion, celery, and ginger until fragrant and the onions are soft.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned. Season with fish sauce.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and water, stir, and bring to a gentle boil.
  5. Sprinkle in the curry powder and let the mixture simmer on low heat until the chicken is tender and the sauce thickens.
  6. Return the fried potatoes to the pan and add the red bell pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning with fish sauce and pepper. Stir in cream if you want a richer sauce.
  8. Transfer to a serving dish and enjoy hot with rice.

Cooking Tips:

  • A small pinch of brown sugar balances the savory flavors.
  • Cook chicken on low heat to keep it tender.
  • Orange bell pepper can be used instead of red for extra color.
  • Leftovers taste great in sandwiches or wraps the next day.
